Sajek Tour Blog

Author: MR.Ashfaq Hossain Raju

Few years back,I have been travling a lot. Whenever i got some vecation or off time i had to go on a tour with my friends. In 2021, I was visited the Bangladesh Most popular place ewhich is called Sajek . It was almost 250 mile far from the dhaka cirty. In sajek there were so much refreshing weather that i could not anywhere.When we are going to enter the Sajek we need a permission from the Army Officer.IN Sajek there only two times the gates open. One is 9.30 Am and another time schedule is 3.00Pm. If you miss the time slote then you need to go back to the bus. Next day you might be able to catch the time slot to visit the sajek.
